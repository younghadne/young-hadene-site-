Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get booked for drill festivals?

Submit demos to promoters, build relationships through social media, and network at industry events. Consistent quality releases and online presence help get noticed.

What should I expect at drill festivals?

Expect high energy crowds, multiple stages, networking opportunities, and potential media coverage. Be prepared for variable conditions and technical challenges.

How long are festival sets typically?

Sets range from 15-45 minutes depending on festival size and artist status. Emerging artists often get shorter slots, while headliners get longer sets.

Do festivals pay artists?

Compensation varies widely. Major festivals pay well, while smaller events may offer exposure over payment. Always clarify terms before committing.

How can I network at festivals?

Attend industry panels, participate in meet-and-greets, exchange contact information, and follow up afterward. Be professional and genuine in interactions.
