Frequently Asked Questions

When did Toronto drill start?

Toronto drill emerged around 2012-2013, building on earlier Toronto hip-hop traditions and influenced by Chicago drill and UK grime.

Who started Toronto drill?

No single person started it, but early pioneers like Drake, PartyNextDoor, and artists from Toronto's underground scene laid the groundwork.

How did Toronto drill become popular?

Viral social media moments, international collaborations, and authentic storytelling resonated with global audiences, leading to mainstream success.

What makes Toronto drill unique?

Toronto drill blends Caribbean rhythms, Canadian perspectives, and urban storytelling with heavy bass and melodic elements.

How has Toronto drill evolved?

From raw underground tracks to polished productions, Toronto drill has incorporated diverse influences while maintaining its core energy and authenticity.
