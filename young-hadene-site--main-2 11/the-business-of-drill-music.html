Frequently Asked Questions

How do drill artists make money?

Primary revenue streams include streaming royalties, live performances, merchandise sales, and brand partnerships. Diversifying income sources is key to financial stability.

When should I get a manager?

Consider management when you have consistent releases, growing fanbase, and need help with bookings, promotion, and business decisions. Good managers add value beyond just representation.

What percentage do managers take?

Standard manager commissions range from 15-25% of gross earnings. The exact percentage depends on experience, services provided, and industry norms.

How do I protect my music rights?

Register your music with performing rights organizations, use copyright notices, and consult entertainment lawyers for contracts. Understanding your rights prevents exploitation.

What are publishing deals?

Publishing deals involve licensing your music for use in media, commercials, and other applications. They can provide steady income beyond performance royalties.
