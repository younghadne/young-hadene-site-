Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Young Hadene?

Young Hadene is Toronto's rising drill music artist with Haitian roots, known for authentic street narratives and innovative production in the Toronto drill scene.

What kind of music does Young Hadene make?

Young Hadene specializes in Toronto drill music, blending trap beats with Haitian cultural influences and storytelling about street life and personal experiences.

Where can I stream Young Hadene's music?

Stream Young Hadene's music on all major platforms including Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, and SoundCloud. Check the streaming page for direct links.

What is Toronto drill?

Toronto drill is a subgenre of drill music originating from Toronto, characterized by heavy 808 bass, melodic elements, and lyrics about street life and local culture.
