About Young Hadene

Young Hadene is a Haitian-born, Toronto-based hip-hop and drill artist known for blending Caribbean influences with Toronto's underground sound. Born on February 10, 1998, and raised between Haiti, Florida, and Toronto, his music reflects resilience, ambition, and sharp storytelling. His notable works include 'Trap Blueprints,' Hood Rich Stories, 'No Choice,' and '30K (LV Tru Diss),' with cinematic visuals and dynamic style. Young Hadene also runs an independent label, Str8hitsRecords, and maintains strong engagement across YouTube, Instagram, SoundCloud, Apple Music, and Spotify.
