About Young Hadene

From Haiti to Toronto

Born in Port-au-Prince, Haiti and raised between Haiti, Florida, and Toronto, Young Hadene brings a unique perspective to the Toronto drill scene.

His music blends Caribbean influences with Toronto's underground sound, creating a unique style that represents the fusion of Haitian musical traditions with contemporary Canadian hip-hop.

Through his independent label Str8hitsRecords, Young Hadene is building infrastructure for Toronto artists while maintaining creative control and authentic cultural representation.
